Ranking a website in Nepal requires a mix of global SEO best practices and local strategies. Below is a concise summary of key steps to improve your Google ranking in the Nepali market.

## 🔍 1. Understand Local Intent
- Target Nepali audiences with **location-specific keywords** (e.g., “best trekking agency in Pokhara”).
- Use **Nepali English** mixed with local terms (e.g., “cheap flight to Kathmandu”).

## 🌐 2. Optimize for .np & Local Hosting
- A `.np` domain or hosting server in Nepal can improve local crawl speed and relevance.

## 📱 3. Mobile-First Indexing
- Most Nepali users browse on mobile. Ensure your site is **responsive**, fast, and easy to navigate on small screens.

## 🗣️ 4. Content in Nepali & English
- Create bilingual content (Nepali + English) to reach a wider audience.
- Use local examples, news, currency (NPR), and addresses.

## 🔗 5. Local Backlinks
- Get backlinks from **Nepali directories**, blogs, news sites (e.g., *Online Khabar*, *The Himalayan Times*), and business listings.

## 🚀 6. Technical SEO
- Improve page speed (use CDN, compress images).
- Implement structured data (local business schema).
- Submit your sitemap to Google Search Console.

## 📍 7. Google My Business & Local SEO
- If you have a physical location in Nepal, create and verify your **Google My Business** profile.
- Encourage customer reviews in Nepali.

## 📢 8. Promote Locally
- Share content on Nepali social platforms (Facebook, TikTok Nepal, YouTube).
- Engage with local communities and forums.

## ✅ Quick Checklist for Nepal-Focused SEO

| Task                          | Status |
|-------------------------------|--------|
| .np domain or local hosting   | ☐      |
| Mobile-optimized design       | ☐      |
| Bilingual content (NE+EN)     | ☐      |
| Local keyword research        | ☐      |
| Google My Business (Nepal)    | ☐      |
| Backlinks from Nepali sites   | ☐      |
| Page speed < 3 sec            | ☐      |
